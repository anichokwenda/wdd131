Chegutu, Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a land locked country in the Southern part of Africa. It is divesified with a lot of Savannah grasslands and vast wildlife. It has four seasons with temperature ranging from 6 degrees Celcius in winters and 35 degrees Celcius in hot summers. We have rain season from end of October to mid May. Main crops grown include maize, wheat , millet and sorghum. I stay in a town next to the capital called Chegutu. It is a farm land where agronomy, horticulture and animal husbandry is common.
